Aperium Automated Trader

Why and How

For cryptocurrency investors who must monitor your positions 24h in a highly volatile environment, our product is a new Automated Trading Platform that provides non-emotional algorithmic based trading decisions to increase profitability. Unlike other trading bots we offer best price for a combination of advanced features, as professional strategies designed by analysts, combined with AI, and the possibility of customer custom configuration.

Aperium trader requires that you have an account on a registered exchange (currently binance.com). During the sign-up process you will get a Trading API Key in your exchange account (follow this guide) and use it to configure Aperium Trader to make buy and sell orders (never withdraw) for selected tokens on your behalf. Select a pricing plan, select a strategy and click Play to start trading, as simple as that for default configuration!
